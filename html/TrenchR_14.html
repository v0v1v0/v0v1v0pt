dec_angle R Documentation

Solar Declination in Radians

Description

The function calculates solar declination, which is the angular distance of the sun north or south of the earthâ€™s equator, based on the day of year (Campbell and Norman 1998).

Usage

dec_angle(doy)

Arguments

doy

numeric day of year (1-366). This can be obtained from standard date via day_of_year.

Value

numeric declination angle (radians).

References

Campbell GS, Norman JM (1998). Introduction to environmental biophysics, 2nd ed. edition. Springer, New York. ISBN 0387949372.
