temperature R Documentation

Temperature in U.S. Cities

Description

Average temperatures for cities in the United States.

  • city Name of the city

  • latitude latitude of the city, in degrees north of the Equator

  • JanTemp mean temperature of the city in January.

  • AprTemp mean temperature of the city in April.

  • AugTemp mean temperature of the city in August.

Format

A data frame with 20 observations on 5 variables.

Source

Mind on Statistics, Fourth Edition, Uts and Heckard.
